## Full Description

**Turn spare minutes into meaningful impact.**

QuickHelp connects people who need small acts of help with willing volunteers in their community — right now, right nearby.

**🤝 HOW IT WORKS**
• Someone needs help: they post a quick request (move boxes, tech help, dog walk, grocery run)
• You see it on the feed or map within seconds
• Tap "Help" → chat → complete the task in 5–30 minutes
• Both parties rate each other — building trust in your community

**⚡ MICRO-VOLUNTEERING**
No long commitments. No sign-ups. No bureaucracy. Just real help, right when it's needed. Every act counts — whether it's 10 minutes or 30.

**🏆 LEVEL UP YOUR IMPACT**
• Earn XP for every task completed
• Unlock 30+ achievement badges
• Maintain daily volunteer streaks
• Climb the local leaderboard
• Complete monthly challenges
• Rise from Newcomer to Community Legend

**📍 HYPERLOCAL**
• See requests within walking or driving distance
• Interactive map with urgency heatmap
• Filter by category, distance, or urgency
• Real-time notifications for nearby requests

**🛡️ SAFE & TRUSTED**
• ID verification system
• AI-powered content moderation
• Bidirectional ratings after every task
• One-tap emergency contact
• 24/7 moderation team

**🌟 REQUEST CATEGORIES**
Elderly Assistance · Tutoring · Food Delivery · Community Cleanup · Pet Help · Tech Support · Transportation · Emergency Tasks · And more

**💬 REAL-TIME COMMUNICATION**
Chat with your helper the moment they accept. Share your location, coordinate arrival, and confirm completion — all in-app.

**📊 TRACK YOUR IMPACT**
See how many hours you've volunteered, tasks completed, and lives touched. Share your impact with friends and inspire others.
